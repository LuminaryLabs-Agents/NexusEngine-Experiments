ThirdPersonFollowThrough

Nexus Engine + Three.js third-person follow kit demo.

WASD move relative to camera. Mouse or arrows yaw/pitch. Space jump. R reset.

Gold sphere = character head/front. Green sphere = look-ahead follow target.

CAMERA MODE
Mode: Smooth Follow
Yaw/Pitch: Control Rotation
Distance: 6.4
Lag: Time-based
