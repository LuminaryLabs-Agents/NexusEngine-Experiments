# SAT-003 — Luminous Nursery

Status: validated / kit-reviewed / preserved
Disposable/preserved root: `experiments/luminous-nursery/`
Selected: 2026-07-19

## Immutable Prompt

Restore three reef cradles by sampling their conditions, correcting each limiting factor, seeding only eligible coral cohorts, and pulsing two deterministic seasons until every nursery is established.

## Semantic Boundary

Primary stress edge: habitat suitability and its limiting-factor evidence gate population migration, then seeded cohort cycles prove stable establishment under authored capacities.

The route owns reef fiction, selected cradle, action ordering, success/failure rules, input, and presentation. It must not own parallel suitability scores, limiting factors, eligibility, population totals, capacity pressure, or seasonal cohort transitions.

## Player Loop

```txt
select cradle -> sample conditions -> tune the limiting factor -> seed eligible cohort
-> repeat for three cradles -> pulse two seasons -> established nursery or contamination loss -> restart
```

## Quality Bar

- Goal and four hero actions are identifiable within five seconds.
- Unsampled, limited, eligible, seeded, and growing states are visible without diagnostics.
- Seeding an ineligible cradle produces a clear loss; correct restoration reaches a clear success.
- Aquarium/nursery presentation is distinct from maps, rescue boards, and council mediation.
- Advanced evidence stays closed by default and restart is deterministic.
