## Gate

A source experiment or superseded game version may be retired only after:

- `feature-migration-ledger.json` inventories mechanics, interactions, scenes, presentation details, and reusable systems;
- every feature has a `keep`, `upgrade`, `merge`, or `drop` disposition with rationale;
- the successor is playable and its feature-migration checklist passes, including valuable environment, water, traversal, combat, objective, progression, and feedback systems;
- catalog, consolidation, route, and documentation references no longer require the source;
- this ledger records the source, successor, proof, commit, and publication result.
